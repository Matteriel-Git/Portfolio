About

I wanted to make a simple and clean time management tool utilizing the Pomodoro Technique. I designed it this way to promote calmness, focus, and efficiency.


  • - 5 different ambient sounds to help focus including a silent option.
  • - Ambient sounds are able to be changed at any time during the session.
  • - Adjustable session lengths and number of breaks.
  • - Audio cue to notify user once session is over.
  • - Keeps count of current session and break.
