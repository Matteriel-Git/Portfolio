About

Become your own professional bartender with this one stop site that provides users with cocktail recipes, cocktail database, ingredient lookup, and an ability to submit your own cocktails.


  • - Browse all the cocktails utilizing the CocktailDB database, sorted by letter.
  • - Get a random cocktail.
  • - Find out all the possible cocktails with a specific ingredient.
  • - Suggest to the webmaster any cocktail to include in the database.
