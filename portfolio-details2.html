About

This React Fitness App has the functionality to choose exercise categories and specific muscle groups, browse thousands of exercises with practical examples, exercise details, pull related videos from youtube, and much more.


  • - Fully responsive for all screen sizes.
  • - Search Exercise, Muscle Group or equipment.
  • - Youtube video suggesions for the detailed overview of exercises.
  • - Recommending exercises based on simmilar muscle group.
  • - Recommending exercises done on simmilar equipment.
